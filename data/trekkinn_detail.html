Product description Salomon Ultra Pro

The Salomon Ultra Pro shoes have been created to run on long distances and versatile terrain, although preferably compact, either dry or wet, where the Wet Traction Contagrip sole grips very well.

In the midsole, we find the EVA and Energy Save compounds, which give it stability and dynamism. Right in the middle of the foot, it has small lugs that increase grip and avoid slips, if you get objects such as rocks or roots.

For this trail running shoe, Salomon opted for a wider last in the fingers, perfect for runners that need to use templates in other shoes.

We must highlight that the Salomon Ultra Pro has a drop of 8mm which gives appropriated cushioning and a stable sensation in each step.

For the upper, the French house used breathable mesh and the D´Haene system for greater support and adjustment, without becoming too tight.

So if you searching protection, dynamism and cushioning in a single running shoe in the mountains, take the Salomon Ultra Pro and put them to the test in any ground.

COPIUOS - 26/04/2020

I have had these for a few months. I do not use them on Ultras or anything close...just running about 25-30 miles per week in between weight/resistance training. I run on the trolley tracks up and down St. Charles Avenue in NEw Orleans where it s hard pack, grass, muddy silt, and through a park circuit in between big Oak trees and meander thorugh the golf course in the park in dewy grass and up and down on undulations. I run at a pretty decent clip and hit as many roots as I can around the bases of the oak trees just for fun. We are below sea level, so the terrain is flat and I try to simulate different types of terrain on my runs to keep it fun and to twist and turn around the root bases of the big oaks. These shoes are really comfortable and do not get waterlogged in the dewy grass at all. It is a mix of gravel-like pavement and the other terrain I mention. I love the wide toe bnox even though I have a skinnier foot. I have a "regular arch and I am not flat footed by any means. I do not "pronate" and run even. These shoes are just great. There is no way to keep your feet from getting somewhat hot in the summer here with 92-97 degree temps and near 100% humidity on a regular basis. Still, I have never felt like my feet overheat. I wea good sox and that helps, too, I suppose. Balanced, stable and a perfect shoe for the mixed terrain I use them on. Great for the gym as well, although I like to wear other shoes to keep these in good shape and avoid any untoward twisting/pressure on toe box and seams. I also do "soccer/rugby/football" style training on the soccer field with synthetic turf. I do all kinds of aerobic calisthenics and I end up doing full wind sprints and cutting drills and the like for an hour or more when the fancy strikes me. These grip the turf and I ca get up to speed quickly. I also run "backwards sprints" in these and they have great traction that way as well. I have never owned a pair of Salamons where the 9.5 did not fit perfect across the board. Hiking shoes, boots, water shoes, and these. I have about 5-6 pair of Salomon shoes and they are all go-to staples and packed for any adventure I plan on undertaking. I have other brands as well that are high quality, although in all my experiences with Salomon products over the last 10 years, I swear by them all. I am about to buy another pair of these shoes, and maybe some Altras just to try out.

Scarpe eccezionali, comode e leggere. Perfette per i lunghi allenamenti. Consigliate.
